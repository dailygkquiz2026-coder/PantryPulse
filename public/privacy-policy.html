Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

PantryPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account (via Google Login), add items to your pantry, or use our AI features. This may include your email address, name, and grocery data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including personalizing your experience and generating AI-powered restocking predictions.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Camera and Permissions

Our app requests access to your camera to scan barcodes and analyze food items. This data is processed locally or via secure AI APIs and is not stored permanently unless you explicitly save a photo.
